## Часть 1. Зачем нужна инверсия

Инверсия (изменённый порядок слов) используется для:
- **Эмфазы** — подчеркнуть важность
- **Академического стиля** — IELTS Writing Task 2
- **Разнообразия** — избежать однообразных структур

Без инверсии: *I have never seen such a thing.*
С инверсией: ***Never have I seen** such a thing.*

---

## Часть 2. Инверсия после отрицательных наречий

| Наречие | Формула | Пример |
|---|---|---|
| **Never** | Never + aux + S + V | **Never have I** seen this before. |
| **Rarely** | Rarely + aux + S + V | **Rarely do we** have such weather. |
| **Seldom** | Seldom + aux + S + V | **Seldom has she** been late. |
| **Hardly** | Hardly + had + S + V3 + when | **Hardly had I** arrived **when** it started. |
| **No sooner** | No sooner + had + S + V3 + than | **No sooner had he** left **than** she called. |
| **Little** | Little + did + S + know | **Little did I** know what would happen. |
| **Not only** | Not only + aux + S + V | **Not only does** it help, but also… |
| **Under no circumstances** | Under no circ. + should + S + V | **Under no circumstances should** you lie. |
| **On no account** | On no account + aux + S + V | **On no account must** you open this. |

---

## Часть 3. Cleft Sentences — выделение

**It is/was … that/who** — выделяем важный элемент:

- Normal: **Education** helps people succeed.
- Cleft: **It is education that** helps people succeed. *(именно образование)*

- Normal: **Hard work** made him successful.
- Cleft: **It was hard work that** made him successful.

Используй в IELTS Writing Task 2 для выделения главной идеи.

---

## Часть 4. What-clauses

**What** + clause + **is/are** — ещё один способ выделить:

- **What we need** is more investment in education.
- **What surprised me** was the speed of change.
- **What I find interesting** is that language shapes thought.

---

## Часть 5. Полезные фразы для IELTS Writing

**Вводные инверсионные фразы:**
- *Not only does this approach benefit individuals, but it also…*
- *Rarely has society faced such complex challenges.*
- *Never before has technology played such a crucial role.*
- *Under no circumstances should governments ignore this issue.*
- *It is through education that real change is achieved.*

**Усиление аргумента:**
- *What is particularly striking is that…*
- *What many people fail to realise is…*
- *It is worth noting that…*
- *It is undeniable that…*

---

## Часть 6. Другие продвинутые структуры

**Participle clauses** (сжатые обороты):
- *Having studied the issue, I concluded that…*
- *Being a language learner myself, I understand the challenges.*

**Reduced relative clauses:**
- The man **standing** by the door = The man **who was standing**…
- The results **obtained** were surprising = The results **which were obtained**…

**Concession (уступительные):**
- *Although technology is helpful, it has its drawbacks.*
- *Despite being expensive, the course was worth it.*
- *While I agree that…, I also believe that…*
- *Even though this may seem straightforward, in practice…*

---

## Для IELTS Speaking (Part 3)

Используй эти структуры в ответах на абстрактные вопросы:
- *"Not only does globalisation bring economic benefits, but it also…"*
- *"What I find most significant is that…"*
- *"It is largely due to technology that we can…"*
- *"Rarely have we seen such rapid change in…"*

Экзаменатор отмечает разнообразие грамматических структур как показатель Band 7+.
