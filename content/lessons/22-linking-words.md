## Часть 1. Зачем нужны linking words

Linking words делают текст **связным и логичным**. Без них аргументы выглядят как несвязный список фактов.

**Без связок:**
> Technology is useful. It can be distracting. People use it too much.

**Со связками:**
> Technology is useful; **however**, it can also be distracting. **As a result**, many people use it too much.

В IELTS Writing, грамотное использование связок = Band 7 за Coherence and Cohesion.

## Часть 2. Главные категории

### Контраст (противопоставление)
| Слово | Структура | Пример |
|---|---|---|
| **however** | However, … / …; however, … | It's hard. **However**, it's worth it. |
| **although** | Although + clause, … | **Although** it's hard, it's worth it. |
| **even though** | Even though + clause, … | **Even though** it's expensive, I bought it. |
| **despite** | Despite + noun/V-ing | **Despite** the cost, I bought it. |
| **in spite of** | In spite of + noun/V-ing | **In spite of** the rain, we walked. |
| **nevertheless** | Nevertheless, … | It was hard. **Nevertheless**, we succeeded. |
| **on the other hand** | On the other hand, … | **On the other hand**, there are benefits. |
| **whereas** | … whereas … | He likes coffee, **whereas** she prefers tea. |
| **while** | While …, … | **While** I agree, I also think… |

### Добавление (дополнительный аргумент)
| Слово | Пример |
|---|---|
| **furthermore** | It saves time. **Furthermore**, it saves money. |
| **moreover** | It's healthy. **Moreover**, it's delicious. |
| **in addition** | **In addition**, we should consider… |
| **besides** | **Besides**, there are other reasons. |
| **also** | It's easy. It's also fun. |

### Причина и следствие
| Слово | Пример |
|---|---|
| **therefore** | She studied hard. **Therefore**, she passed. |
| **as a result** | **As a result**, sales increased. |
| **consequently** | **Consequently**, the project was delayed. |
| **because of** | **Because of** the rain, we stayed home. |
| **due to** | **Due to** budget cuts, staff were fired. |
| **since** | **Since** he was tired, he went to bed. |
| **because** | I stayed home **because** it was raining. |

### Примеры и иллюстрации
| Слово | Пример |
|---|---|
| **for example** | **For example**, Paris is very expensive. |
| **for instance** | **For instance**, learning music improves memory. |
| **such as** | Cities **such as** London have high rents. |
| **in particular** | This applies, **in particular**, to teenagers. |

### Обобщение и вывод
| Слово | Пример |
|---|---|
| **in conclusion** | **In conclusion**, technology is changing society. |
| **to sum up** | **To sum up**, there are both advantages and disadvantages. |
| **overall** | **Overall**, the benefits outweigh the drawbacks. |
| **in other words** | **In other words**, we need to act now. |
| **that is to say** | It's not expensive, **that is to say**, it's affordable. |

## Часть 3. Топ-ошибки

| ❌ Ошибка | ✅ Верно |
|---|---|
| Although…but… | **Although** it's hard. / It's hard, **but**… |
| Despite + clause | **Despite** the rain. (noun) / **Although** it rained. |
| However + запятая | However**,** I agree. (с запятой!) |
| Because of + clause | **Because of** the problem. / **Because** there was a problem. |

---

## IELTS Writing шаблон (Task 2)

```
Intro: В целом… (paraphrase the question)
Body 1: Firstly, … Furthermore, …  For example, …
Body 2: However, … On the other hand, … Consequently, …
Conclusion: In conclusion, … Overall, …
```
