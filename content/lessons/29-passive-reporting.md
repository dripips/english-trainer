## Глаголы мнения/сообщения

| Глагол | Пассивная форма | Значение |
|---|---|---|
| say | **is said to** / **it is said that** | говорят, что |
| believe | **is believed to** / **it is believed that** | считается, что |
| think | **is thought to** / **it is thought that** | думают, что |
| expect | **is expected to** / **it is expected that** | ожидается, что |
| report | **is reported to** / **it is reported that** | сообщается, что |
| know | **is known to** / **it is known that** | известно, что |
| consider | **is considered to be** / **it is considered that** | считается |
| claim | **is claimed to** / **it is claimed that** | утверждается |
| allege | **is alleged to** / **it is alleged that** | предполагается (юридич.) |
| find | **is found to** / **it is found that** | установлено, что |

---

## Две структуры

**Структура 1: It + passive verb + that-clause**
```
It is believed     that the economy will recover.
It is thought      that the policy has failed.
It is reported     that thousands were affected.
```

**Структура 2: Subject + passive verb + to-infinitive**
```
The economy  is believed  to recover next year.
The policy   is thought   to have failed.
Thousands    are reported to have been affected.
```

> **Прошлое в структуре 2:** Subject + is said **to have + V3**
> - He is said **to have stolen** the painting.
> - She is believed **to have been** living abroad.

---

## Зачем это нужно в IELTS

В академическом эссе не всегда уместно писать «I think» или «Scientists say». Пассивный залог с глаголами сообщения звучит **объективно и академично**:

❌ Many people think climate change is a serious problem.  
✅ Climate change **is widely considered** to be a serious problem.

❌ Some scientists say that the cure will be found soon.  
✅ **It is claimed that** a cure may be found in the near future.

## Полезные усилители

| Наречие | Значение | Пример |
|---|---|---|
| **widely** | широко | widely believed/accepted |
| **generally** | в целом | generally considered |
| **commonly** | повсеместно | commonly known |
| **often** | часто | often thought |
| **strongly** | настойчиво | strongly argued |
