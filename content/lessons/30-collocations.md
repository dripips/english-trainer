## MAKE vs DO — главное правило

**MAKE** = создавать что-то новое, производить результат  
**DO** = выполнять действие, задачу, работу

### MAKE
| Коллокация | Пример |
|---|---|
| make a **mistake** | Don't make the same mistake twice. |
| make a **decision** | She made a difficult decision. |
| make **progress** | He's making great progress. |
| make an **effort** | Please make an effort to arrive on time. |
| make a **difference** | Your help makes a real difference. |
| make **money** | He makes a lot of money. |
| make a **suggestion** | Can I make a suggestion? |
| make a **noise** | Stop making so much noise! |
| make a **phone call** | I need to make a quick call. |
| make a **list** | Let's make a list of everything we need. |
| make a **speech** | He made a wonderful speech at the wedding. |
| make **friends** | She finds it easy to make friends. |

### DO
| Коллокация | Пример |
|---|---|
| do **homework** | Have you done your homework? |
| do **housework** | I need to do the housework. |
| do **exercise** | He does exercise every morning. |
| do **your best** | Just do your best. |
| do **a favour** | Can you do me a favour? |
| do **good / harm** | Smoking does a lot of harm. |
| do **the shopping** | She does the shopping on Saturdays. |
| do **research** | Scientists are doing research into cancer. |
| do **business** | We do business with over 50 countries. |
| do **a course** | She's doing a course in marketing. |

## HAVE vs TAKE

### HAVE
| Коллокация | Пример |
|---|---|
| have **breakfast/lunch/dinner** | I usually have breakfast at 7. |
| have a **shower / bath** (UK) | He has a shower every morning. |
| have a **conversation** | We had an interesting conversation. |
| have a **meeting** | We're having a meeting at 3 p.m. |
| have **fun / a good time** | Did you have fun at the party? |
| have a **look** | Can I have a look at your notes? |
| have a **rest** | You look tired — have a rest. |
| have a **go** | Let me have a go at fixing it. |
| have an **argument** | They had an argument about money. |
| have a **problem** | I'm having a problem with my computer. |

### TAKE
| Коллокация | Пример |
|---|---|
| take a **shower** (US/neutral) | I take a shower every evening. |
| take a **break** | Let's take a break! |
| take a **photo** | Can I take a photo of you? |
| take **notes** | Please take notes during the lecture. |
| take a **look** | Take a look at this! |
| take **care of** | Who will take care of the children? |
| take **part in** | She took part in the competition. |
| take **action** | The government needs to take action. |
| take **time** | Learning a language takes time. |
| take a **taxi / bus** | I'll take a taxi to the airport. |

---

## GO + спорт и активности

**go + V-ing** для спортивных активностей:
- go **swimming** / **running** / **cycling** / **skiing** / **hiking**

**go for a** + существительное:
- go for a **walk** / **run** / **swim** / **drive** / **ride**
