    In recent years, social media platforms have become an integral part of modern life. While some argue that these platforms are harmful, I believe that social media has both advantages and disadvantages, though the benefits outweigh the drawbacks.

    On the one hand, social media can have a detrimental effect on mental health. Furthermore, the spread of misinformation on social media poses a significant threat to society.

    On the other hand, social media enables people to stay connected with friends and family across the globe. It also provides a platform for sharing information, raising awareness of important issues, and supporting social movements. In addition, businesses can reach a global audience at relatively low cost.

    In conclusion, although social media presents certain risks, I believe that when used responsibly, its benefits to communication and society are considerable. Governments and technology companies should work together to minimise the negative effects.

## Типы эссе в IELTS Task 2

| Тип | Формулировка в задании | Структура |
|---|---|---|
| **Agree / Disagree** | To what extent do you agree? | Intro(позиция) → Body1(за) → Body2(против) → Conclusion |
| **Discuss both views** | Discuss both views and give your opinion | Intro → Body1(view1) → Body2(view2+мнение) → Conclusion |
| **Advantages / Disadvantages** | Discuss the advantages and disadvantages | Intro → Body1(adv) → Body2(dis) → Conclusion |
| **Problem / Solution** | What are the problems? What solutions can you suggest? | Intro → Body1(problems) → Body2(solutions) → Conclusion |
| **Two-part question** | Why is this? What can be done? | Intro → Body1(причины) → Body2(решения) → Conclusion |

---

## Структура Introduction (2–3 предложения)

**1. Paraphrase** — перефразируй тему (не копируй!):
> *"In today's world, social media is an indispensable part of daily life."*

**2. Thesis statement** — твоя позиция:
> *"Although social media has certain drawbacks, I believe its benefits to communication outweigh the negatives."*

---

## Структура Body paragraph (PEEL)

| Буква | Что делать | Пример |
|---|---|---|
| **P** — Point | Тема абзаца | "One significant advantage of remote work is flexibility." |
| **E** — Explain | Объясни | "Employees can manage their own time and work from any location." |
| **E** — Evidence | Пример/факт | "For instance, studies show that remote workers are 20% more productive." |
| **L** — Link | Свяжи с тезисом | "This demonstrates that flexible working arrangements benefit both employees and employers." |

## Фразы для эссе

### Введение
- In recent years / decades, …
- It is widely believed that …
- There is growing concern about …
- The issue of … has become increasingly important.

### Добавление аргумента
- Furthermore, / Moreover, / In addition,
- Another key point is that …
- It is also worth noting that …

### Контраст / Counterargument
- However, / Nevertheless,
- On the other hand, / In contrast,
- Although / Even though / Despite this,
- Critics argue that … ; however, …

### Примеры
- For example, / For instance,
- This is illustrated by …
- A clear example of this is …
- Research suggests / indicates that …

### Вывод
- In conclusion, / To conclude, / To sum up,
- Overall, it is clear that …
- Taking everything into account, …
- I would argue / maintain that …
