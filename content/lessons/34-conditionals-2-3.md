reading:
  textEn: |
    Regrets and Dreams

    People often think about what might have been. If I had worked harder at school, I would have gone to university. But I didn't, and I didn't. That's the third conditional — looking back at the past with regret.

    Then there's the second conditional, for imagining the present differently. If I had more money right now, I would buy a flat. I don't have more money, so I won't. It's not real — just a dream.

    The mixed conditional combines both: If I had studied medicine (past — I didn't), I would be a doctor now (present — I'm not). The cause is in the past, but the result affects now.

    One useful phrase: "I wish I had…" — the same idea as the third conditional. "I wish I had listened to my parents." Same as "If I had listened to my parents, things would have been different."

## 2nd Conditional — нереально сейчас

**Форма:** If + Past Simple, would + V (infinitive)

| Условие (нереально) | Результат |
|---|---|
| If I **had** more money, | I **would buy** a flat. |
| If she **lived** in London, | she **would see** us more often. |
| If it **wasn't** raining, | we **would go** for a walk. |

> **Правило:** В if-части используем Past Simple, но это **не** прошедшее время — это нереальность. *If I were you…* (формально: **were**, не was)

---

## 3rd Conditional — нереально в прошлом

**Форма:** If + **had** + V3, **would have** + V3

| Условие (нереально, прошлое) | Результат (нереальный) |
|---|---|
| If I **had studied** more, | I **would have passed**. |
| If she **hadn't missed** the bus, | she **would have been** on time. |
| If we **had left** earlier, | we **wouldn't have been** stuck. |

---

## Mixed Conditional

**Форма:** If + **had** + V3 (прошлое), **would** + V (настоящее)

> *If I had studied medicine, I **would be** a doctor now.*
> (Не изучал → сейчас не врач)

> *If he hadn't broken his leg, he **would be** playing in tomorrow's match.*

---

## I wish / If only

| Фраза | Смысл |
|---|---|
| I wish I **had** more time. | 2nd cond. — нереально сейчас |
| I wish I **had studied** harder. | 3rd cond. — сожаление о прошлом |
| If only I **hadn't said** that! | то же, но сильнее |

---

## Частые ошибки

| ❌ Ошибка | ✅ Правильно |
|---|---|
| If I **would have** more money… | If I **had** more money… |
| If she **would study** harder… | If she **studied** / **had studied**… |
| I wish I **studied** harder yesterday. | I wish I **had studied** harder yesterday. |
