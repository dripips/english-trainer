## Сдвиг времён (Tense Backshift)

| Прямая речь | Косвенная речь |
|---|---|
| Present Simple: *"I **work**…"* | Past Simple: *she said she **worked**…* |
| Present Continuous: *"I **am going**…"* | Past Continuous: *he said he **was going**…* |
| Present Perfect: *"I **have seen**…"* | Past Perfect: *she said she **had seen**…* |
| Past Simple: *"I **went**…"* | Past Perfect: *he said he **had gone**…* |
| will: *"I **will** call…"* | would: *she said she **would** call…* |
| can: *"I **can** help…"* | could: *he said he **could** help…* |
| must: *"I **must** go…"* | had to: *she said she **had to** go…* |

## Смена местоимений и наречий

| Прямая речь | Косвенная речь |
|---|---|
| I, my, me | he/she, his/her, him/her |
| we, our, us | they, their, them |
| here | there |
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| yesterday | the day before |
| tomorrow | the next day / the following day |
| this week | that week |
| next week | the following week |

---

## Вопросы в косвенной речи

**Да/нет вопросы:** asked **if** / **whether**
> "Are you hungry?" → She asked **if I was** hungry. *(НЕТ инверсии!)*

**Специальные вопросы:** asked + вопросное слово
> "Where do you live?" → He asked **where I lived**. *(НЕТ do/does/did!)*

## Команды и просьбы

| Прямая речь | Косвенная речь |
|---|---|
| "**Open** the window." | told me **to open** |
| "**Don't** touch that!" | told me **not to touch** |
| "**Please help** me." | asked me **to help** |

## say vs tell

| | say | tell |
|---|---|---|
| С объектом | ❌ She **said me**… | ✅ She **told me**… |
| Без объекта | ✅ She **said** (that)… | ❌ She **told** (that)… |
| С цитатой | ✅ She **said**, "Hello." | — |
