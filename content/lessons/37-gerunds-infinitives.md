  textEn: |
    Different Meanings

    The words "stop", "remember", "try" and "forget" can be followed by either a gerund (-ing) or an infinitive (to + V) — and the meaning changes completely.

    I stopped smoking last year. (I no longer smoke.)
    I stopped to smoke. (I stopped what I was doing in order to have a cigarette.)

    I remember meeting her at the party. (I have a memory of meeting her — it already happened.)
    Remember to call your mum! (Don't forget — you need to do it in the future.)

    I tried taking the medication, but it didn't help. (I did it as an experiment.)
    I tried to open the door, but it was locked. (I made an effort, but failed.)

    With most verbs, however, you just need to learn the pattern: enjoy doing, want to do, suggest doing, decide to do.

## Только + V-ing (герундий)

```
enjoy   avoid   finish   mind   suggest
miss    keep    consider  imagine  can't help
practise  deny   give up  put off  look forward to
```

> I **enjoy** swimming. / She **avoids** eating sugar. / He **keeps** interrupting me.

---

## Только + to V (инфинитив)

```
want    need    decide  hope   plan
agree   refuse  offer   manage  fail
expect  promise  afford  tend   would like
```

> I **want** to leave. / She **decided** to stay. / He **refused** to help.

---

## И то, и другое — без изменения смысла

```
like / love / hate / prefer / begin / start / continue
```

> I **love cooking** = I **love to cook**. *(практически одинаково)*

---

## Смена смысла — важно!

| | + V-ing | + to V |
|---|---|---|
| **stop** | I stopped **smoking**. *(прекратил)* | I stopped **to smoke**. *(остановился чтобы)* |
| **remember** | I remember **meeting** her. *(помню как)* | Remember **to call**! *(не забудь)* |
| **forget** | I forgot **locking** the door. *(забыл, что уже)* | I forgot **to lock** the door. *(забыл и не сделал)* |
| **try** | Try **taking** aspirin. *(попробуй как эксперимент)* | I tried **to open** it. *(пытался, м.б. безуспешно)* |
| **regret** | I regret **saying** that. *(сожалею, что сказал)* | I regret **to inform** you… *(сожалею, что должен сообщить)* |

---

## Особые случаи

**to** как предлог (после него V-ing!)
- look forward **to seeing** you
- I'm used **to living** alone
- object **to doing** something
- in addition **to working**, she studies

**make / let / help + bare infinitive (без to)**
- She **made** me **do** it.
- Let me **help** you.
- He **helped** her **carry** the bags.
