  textEn: |
    Why Stress Matters

    Word stress is crucial for being understood in English. If you stress the wrong syllable, native speakers may not understand you — even if every other aspect of your pronunciation is perfect.

    Consider the word "economy". We say e-CON-o-my. But "economic" shifts: e-co-NOM-ic. And "economics": e-co-NOM-ics. The stress moves with the suffix.

    Some words change their meaning — and their stress — depending on whether they're a noun or a verb: a REcord (noun) vs. to reCORD (verb). The same applies to: PROtest / proTEST, OBject / obJECT, PERmit / perMIT.

    Suffixes are your friend: -TION, -SION always stress the syllable before: inforMAtion, educaTION, communicATION. Similarly, -ITY: abilITY, opportunITY, possibilITY. And -IC: draMAtic, econOMic, realiSTIC.

## Правила ударения

### Суффиксы — ударение на слог ПЕРЕД

| Суффикс | Примеры |
|---|---|
| **-TION / -SION** | informATion, educATion, communicATion, decISion |
| **-ITY** | abilITy, opportunITy, possibilITy, univerSITy |
| **-IC** | draMAtic, econOMic, realiSTic, photoGRAPHic |
| **-ICAL** | poLITical, econOMical, biologIcal |
| **-IOUS / -EOUS** | amBItious, couRAgeous, outRAGeous |

---

### Существительное vs Глагол (двусложные)

| Существительное (1-й слог) | Глагол (2-й слог) |
|---|---|
| **RE**cord | re**CORD** |
| **PRO**test | pro**TEST** |
| **OB**ject | ob**JECT** |
| **PER**mit | per**MIT** |
| **RE**fund | re**FUND** |
| **CON**flict | con**FLICT** |
| **IN**crease | in**CREASE** |
| **EX**port | ex**PORT** |

### Слова, которые часто произносят неправильно

| Слово | ❌ Неверно | ✅ Верно |
|---|---|---|
| comfortable | comFORTable | **COM**fortable |
| interesting | inteRESTing | **IN**teresting |
| vegetable | vегеTAble | **VEG**etable |
| different | difFErent | **DIF**ferent |
| necessary | neCESSary | **NEC**essary |
| photograph | phoTOgraph | **PHO**tograph |
| economy | ECOnomy | e**CON**omy |

## Для IELTS Speaking

Неправильное ударение снижает **Pronunciation** band (25% итоговой оценки).

Проверяй в Cambridge Dictionary → кнопка 🔊 → смотри на **ˈ** перед ударным слогом:
- **ˈ**pho.to.graph = PHOtograph  
- pho**ˈ**to.gra.phy = phoTOgraphy  
- pho.to**ˈ**gra.phic = photoGRAPHic
