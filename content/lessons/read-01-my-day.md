reading:
  textEn: |
    My name is Anna. I am thirty years old, and I live in a small flat in the
    city. Every day is the same for me, and I like that. I get up at seven
    o'clock. First, I open the window and look at the sky. Then I wash my face
    and brush my teeth. After that, I eat breakfast in the kitchen. I drink hot
    tea and eat bread with cheese. Sometimes I eat an apple too.

    I go to work at eight o'clock. I walk to work because the shop is near my
    home. I work in a small shop. We sell bread, milk, fruit, and many other
    things. I like my work because I talk to many people every day. My boss is
    kind, and my friend Kate works with me.

    At one o'clock I have lunch with Kate. We eat hot soup and bread. We talk
    about our families and our plans. We laugh a lot. After lunch, I work again
    until five o'clock.

    I come home at six o'clock. I am a little tired, but I am happy. In the
    evening, I cook dinner. I like to cook pasta or rice with vegetables. Then I
    read a book or watch TV on the sofa. Sometimes I call my mother on the phone.
    We talk for a long time. She lives in another city, and I miss her.

    At ten o'clock I go to bed. I close my eyes and think about my day. I am
    happy because my life is calm and good. This is my normal day, and tomorrow
    will be the same.

    часов.
