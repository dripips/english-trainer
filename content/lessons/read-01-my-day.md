    My name is Anna. I get up at seven o'clock every day. First I wash my face and brush my teeth. Then I eat breakfast. I drink tea and eat bread with cheese. I go to work at eight. I work in a small shop. I like my work. At one o'clock I have lunch with my friend Kate. We eat soup and talk. I come home at six. In the evening I cook dinner and read a book. Sometimes I watch TV.
