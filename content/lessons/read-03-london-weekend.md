  textEn: |
    Last weekend my sister and I travelled to London. We took an early train
    on Saturday morning, and we arrived in the city at ten o'clock.
    The weather was cold, but the sun was bright.
    First, we walked along the river. We saw Big Ben and took many photos.
    The clock was very tall and beautiful. After that, we visited a big museum.
    We looked at old paintings and ancient statues for two hours.
    I really liked the Egyptian room.
    At lunchtime, we were hungry, so we found a small cafe near the museum.
    I ate fish and chips, and my sister had a sandwich. The food was delicious.
    In the afternoon, we went shopping and bought some gifts for our parents.
    We felt tired but very happy. In the evening, we caught the train home.
    It was a short trip, but I loved every minute of it.
    I want to go back to London again next year.
