    Tom started a new job last Monday. He works at a small company in the
    city centre. Now he is a junior designer. He was very nervous on his
    first day, but everything went well.
    In the morning, Tom arrived at the office at nine o'clock. His new boss,
    Mrs Park, was kind and friendly. She showed him his desk and his computer.
    Then she introduced him to his colleagues. Everyone said hello and smiled.
    Tom's first task was easy. He had to read some files and learn about the
    projects. After lunch, he helped a colleague with a poster. He felt happy
    because the work was interesting.
    Now Tom likes his new job. The office is bright and quiet, and his team is
    friendly. Every day he learns something new. Next month, he wants to start
    a bigger project. He hopes to become a good designer one day.
