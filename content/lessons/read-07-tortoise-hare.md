  textEn: |
    A tortoise and a hare live in the forest. The hare is very fast. The tortoise
    is very slow. The hare laughs at the tortoise. "You are so slow!" he says.
    One day the tortoise says, "Let us run a race." The hare laughs again. "OK,"
    he says. "I can win." All the animals come to watch.
    The race starts. The hare runs very fast. The tortoise walks slow. Soon the
    hare is far in front. "I am so fast," he thinks. "I can sleep now."
    The hare sleeps under a tree. But the tortoise does not stop. He walks and
    walks. He is slow, but he does not stop.
    The hare wakes up. He runs fast to the end. But it is too late! The tortoise
    is there. The tortoise wins the race! Slow and steady wins the race.
