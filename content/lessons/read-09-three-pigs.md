  textEn: |
    Once there were three little pigs. They wanted new houses.
    The first pig made a house of straw. It was fast and easy.
    The second pig made a house of sticks. It was also fast.
    The third pig made a house of bricks. It was slow and hard.
    A big wolf came to the first house. "Little pig, let me in!"
    "No, no!" said the pig. The wolf began to huff and puff.
    He huffed and he puffed, and he blew the straw house down.
    The pig ran to the second house. The wolf came again.
    He huffed and he puffed, and he blew the stick house down.
    The two pigs ran to the brick house. The wolf came again.
    He huffed and he puffed, but the brick house did not fall.
    The wolf was tired and went away. The three pigs
    were happy and safe in the strong house.
