reading:
  textEn: |
    Once upon a time, a little girl lived near a big forest. She always wore a
    red coat with a hood, so everyone called her Little Red Riding Hood.
    One morning her mother said, "Grandmother is not well. Please take this
    basket of bread and cakes to her house. Walk on the path and do not talk to
    strangers."
    The little girl walked into the forest. Soon she met a big wolf. "Where are
    you going?" asked the wolf with a smile. The girl forgot her mother's words
    and answered, "I am going to my grandmother's house."
    The wolf ran fast to the house. He locked Grandmother in the cupboard and put
    on her clothes. When the girl arrived, she looked at the wolf in the bed.
    "Grandmother, what big teeth you have!" she said.
    A kind woodcutter was working near the house. He heard the girl shout, so he
    ran inside with his axe. The wolf was afraid and jumped out of the window. The
    woodcutter opened the cupboard, and Grandmother was safe.
    Little Red Riding Hood hugged her grandmother. After that day, she always
    listened to her mother and never talked to strangers again.
