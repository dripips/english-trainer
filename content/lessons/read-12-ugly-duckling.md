  textEn: |
    One warm summer, a mother duck sat on her eggs near the water. She waited
    for many days. At last the eggs opened one by one, and little yellow
    ducklings came out. "How sweet they are!" said the mother duck. But one egg
    was bigger than the others, and it opened last. The baby inside was grey and
    very big. "What an ugly duckling!" the other birds laughed.

    From that day, the poor duckling was very lonely. His brothers and sisters
    did not play with him. The hens and the cat on the farm laughed at him and
    pushed him away. Even the girl who gave them food did not like him, and she
    chased him with her foot. He was so sad and unhappy that he decided to
    leave. So he said goodbye to no one. He left the farm and walked out into
    the wide world all alone.

    The duckling lived by a cold lake. Autumn came, and then winter came too. It
    was very cold, and snow fell on the water. The duckling was hungry, tired,
    and afraid, but he did not give up. He waited, and he hoped for better days.

    When spring came at last, the sun was warm again. One day the duckling saw
    three beautiful white birds on the lake. They were swans, the most beautiful
    birds of all. "I want to be near them," he thought, "but they are too
    beautiful for an ugly bird like me." Slowly he swam to the water and looked
    down at his face. He could not believe his eyes! He was not grey and ugly
    any more. He was a beautiful white swan too!

    The duckling was never an ugly duckling at all. He was a young swan, and now
    he was fully grown up. The other swans swam to him and welcomed him.
    Children stood by the lake and said, "Look, the new swan is the most
    beautiful of them all!" And so the young swan was happy at last.
