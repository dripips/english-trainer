reading:
  textEn: |
    One summer a mother duck sat on her eggs near the water. The eggs opened, and
    little yellow ducklings came out. But one egg was big, and the last baby was
    grey and very big. "What an ugly duckling!" the other birds said.

    The poor duckling was lonely. The other animals laughed at him and pushed him
    away. So he left the farm and walked into the world alone. Winter came, and it
    was cold. The duckling was hungry and sad, but he did not give up.

    When spring came, he saw three beautiful white birds on the lake. They were
    swans. "I want to be near them," he thought, "but they are too beautiful for
    me." He went to the water and looked down. He saw his face in the water — and
    he was a beautiful white swan too!

    He was not an ugly duckling. He was a young swan, and now he was grown up.
    The other swans came to him. Children said, "Look, the new swan is the most
    beautiful of all!" The swan was very happy at last.
