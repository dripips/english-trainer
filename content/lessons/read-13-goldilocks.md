reading:
  textEn: |
    Once there was a little girl. Her name was Goldilocks. One day she walked in
    the forest and found a small house. The door was open, so she went inside.
    On the table she saw three bowls of porridge. Goldilocks was hungry. She
    tried the big bowl, but it was too hot. She tried the middle bowl, but it
    was too cold. Then she tried the small bowl. It was just right, so she ate
    it all.
    After that she saw three chairs. The big chair was too hard. The middle
    chair was too soft. The small chair was just right, but it broke!
    Goldilocks was tired now. She went upstairs and found three beds. The big
    bed was too high. The middle bed was too low. The small bed was just right,
    so she went to sleep.
    Soon the three bears came home. "Someone ate my porridge!" said the baby
    bear. They went upstairs and saw Goldilocks. She woke up, jumped from the
    bed, and ran home very fast.
