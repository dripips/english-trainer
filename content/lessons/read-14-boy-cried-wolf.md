reading:
  textEn: |
    Long ago, a young shepherd boy lived near a small village. Every day he
    took the sheep up the hill. The work was quiet and boring, and the boy was
    often alone. One afternoon he had an idea for some fun.
    He ran down to the village and shouted, "Wolf! Wolf! A wolf is eating the
    sheep!" The people heard him and ran up the hill with sticks. But there was
    no wolf. The boy only laughed at them. The people were angry and walked
    home.
    The next day the boy was bored again. So he shouted, "Wolf! Wolf!" once
    more. Again the people ran up the hill, and again there was no wolf. "Stop
    your lies!" they said. They did not like this boy at all.
    Then, one evening, a real wolf came. It ran among the sheep. The boy was
    afraid and shouted, "Wolf! Wolf! Please help me!" But this time nobody
    moved. The people only said, "He is a liar again." So the wolf ate many
    sheep, and the boy learned a hard lesson.

    На следующий день мальчику снова было скучно. Поэтому он опять закричал:
    «Волк! Волк!» Снова люди побежали на холм, и снова волка не было.
    «Хватит врать!» — сказали они. Этот мальчик им совсем не нравился.
    Потом, однажды вечером, пришёл настоящий волк. Он бегал среди овец. Мальчик
