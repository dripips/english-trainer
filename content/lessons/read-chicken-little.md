  textEn: |
    One sunny day, Chicken Little sat under a big tree.
    She looked for food in the grass. The day was warm and quiet.
    Then an acorn fell from the tree. It fell right on her head. Bonk!
    "Ouch!" said Chicken Little. She looked up, but she did not see the acorn.
    "Oh no! The sky is falling!" said Chicken Little.
    "This is bad. Very, very bad. I must go and tell the king!"
    So she ran and ran down the road.
    Soon she met Henny Penny the hen.
    "Where are you going so fast?" asked Henny Penny.
    "The sky is falling!" said Chicken Little. "I must tell the king!"
    "Oh no!" said Henny Penny. "The sky is falling! I will come too."
    So they ran and ran together.
    Then they met Ducky Lucky the duck.
    "Where are you going so fast?" asked Ducky Lucky.
    "The sky is falling!" they said. "We must tell the king!"
    "Oh no! I will come too," said the duck.
    So they ran and ran together.
    Then they met Goosey Loosey the goose.
    "Where are you going so fast?" asked Goosey Loosey.
    "The sky is falling!" they said. "We must tell the king!"
    "Oh no! I will come too," said the goose.
    So they all ran and ran to tell the king.
    They were very afraid. They thought the sky was coming down.
    But there was no king. And the sky was not falling.
    It was only a little acorn from the big tree!
    They looked at each other. Then they began to laugh.
    "We were all very silly!" they said.
    And they walked home happy under the blue sky.
