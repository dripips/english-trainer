  textEn: |
    It is a very hot summer day. The sun is big and yellow in the sky.
    A black crow flies over the fields. She is very thirsty. She wants water.
    "I need a drink," she says. "Where is the water?"
    The crow flies and flies. She looks for water everywhere.
    She looks near the trees. She looks near the houses. But there is no water.
    The crow is tired. Her wings hurt. She is hot and thirsty.
    Then she sees something. It is a tall pitcher in a garden.
    The crow flies down. She looks inside the pitcher.
    There is some water at the bottom. "Water! Good water!" she says. She is very happy.
    The crow puts her head in the pitcher. She wants to drink.
    But the pitcher is tall, and the water is low. She cannot reach it.
    She tries again and again. She cannot drink. The water is too far down.
    The crow is sad. "What can I do?" she thinks.
    She does not fly away. She sits and thinks and thinks.
    Then she sees small stones on the ground near the pitcher.
    The crow has a good idea! "I know!" she says.
    She takes one stone in her beak and drops it in the pitcher. Plop!
    She takes another stone, and another, and another.
    One by one, she puts many stones in the pitcher.
    Slowly the water comes up. It comes higher and higher.
    Now the water is near the top. The crow can reach it!
    She puts her head in and drinks the cool, fresh water.
    The clever crow is very happy. "Now I am not thirsty," she says.
    Then she opens her wings and flies away.

    Ворона спускается вниз. Она смотрит внутрь кувшина.

    Ворона засовывает голову в кувшин. Она хочет попить.
    Но кувшин высокий, а вода низко. Она не может до неё дотянуться.
    Она пробует снова и снова. Она не может попить. Вода слишком глубоко.

    У вороны появляется хорошая идея! «Я знаю!» — говорит она.

    Теперь вода у самого верха. Ворона может до неё дотянуться!

    Потом она расправляет крылья и улетает.
