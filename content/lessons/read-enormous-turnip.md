reading:
  textEn: |
    In the spring, an old man planted a little turnip in his garden.
    He gave it water every day. He said, "Grow, grow, little turnip!
    Grow big and sweet and strong."
    The little turnip began to grow. It grew and grew.
    It grew big. It grew very, very big. Soon it was enormous!
    One morning the old man wanted to pull the turnip up.
    He took the turnip in his two hands. He pulled and pulled,
    but he could not pull it up. The turnip was too big.
    So the old man called the old woman.
    The old woman pulled the old man, and the old man pulled the turnip.
    They pulled and pulled, but they could not pull it up.
    Then they called the little girl.
    The girl pulled the old woman, the old woman pulled the old man,
    and the old man pulled the turnip.
    They pulled and pulled, but they could not pull it up.
    Then they called the dog. The dog pulled the girl.
    They pulled and pulled, but they could not pull it up.
    Then they called the cat. The cat pulled the dog.
    Still they could not pull it up.
    At last they called the little mouse.
    The mouse pulled the cat, the cat pulled the dog, the dog pulled the girl,
    the girl pulled the old woman, the old woman pulled the old man,
    and the old man pulled the turnip.
    They all pulled together — one, two, three!
    And up came the enormous turnip!
    Everybody was happy. That night they had a big, sweet turnip for dinner.

    но не смог её вытянуть. Репка была слишком большая.
