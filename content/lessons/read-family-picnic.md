  textEn: |
    It is a sunny Saturday in summer. The Brown family wants to have a picnic.
    "Let's go to the park!" says Dad. Everybody is happy.
    Mom makes sandwiches with cheese and tomato. She puts them in a basket.
    Dad takes red apples, some cake, and a big bottle of water.
    Lily takes a big red blanket. Tom takes his new ball.
    They walk to the park together. The sky is blue and warm.
    Birds sing in the trees. The park is green and beautiful.
    They find a good place under a big tree.
    They put the blanket on the soft green grass.
    The family sits down and eats lunch. The sandwiches are very good.
    "Mmm, I like this cheese!" says Tom. Everybody laughs.
    After lunch, Tom and Dad play with the ball. They run and jump.
    Lily and Mom look at the flowers. They see red, yellow, and white flowers.
    Then a small brown dog runs to them. The dog wants to play too!
    Tom throws the ball, and the dog runs after it. It is so funny.
    In the afternoon the sun goes down a little. The family is tired but happy.
    They put everything back in the bag and the basket.
    Then they walk home slowly.
    "What a nice day!" says Mom. "Yes, the best day!" says Lily.
