reading:
  textEn: |
    A man and his wife live on a small farm. They have a big white goose.
    One morning the man goes to the goose. He wants to take an egg.
    He sees an egg in the nest. But this egg is not white.
    It is yellow. It shines in the sun. It is gold!
    The man takes the golden egg in his hands. He is very, very happy.
    He runs to the town and sells the egg. He gets a lot of money.
    The next morning the goose gives another golden egg.
    Every morning there is one new golden egg in the nest.
    The man sells the eggs, one by one. Soon the man and his wife are rich.
    They have a big house and fine clothes.
    But the man wants more. He is not happy with one egg a day.
    "One egg a day is too slow," he says. "I want all the gold now!"
    He thinks, "There must be a lot of gold inside the goose."
    So one day the man kills the goose. He looks inside.
    But there is no gold inside. The goose is like any other goose.
    Now the goose is dead. There are no more golden eggs. Not one.
    The man is sad and poor again. He cries, "Oh, what did I do?"
    The wise wife says, "You wanted too much, and now you have nothing."
