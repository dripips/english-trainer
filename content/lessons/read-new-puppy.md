reading:
  textEn: |
    Today is a special day for Jack. He is seven years old. The family gets a
    new puppy. Jack is very happy. He waits and waits.
    In the morning, they go to the farm. The sun is warm and the sky is blue.
    At the farm there are six little dogs. They are small and soft.
    One little dog is black. Two dogs are white. Three dogs are brown.
    The dogs play and run. They are very cute.
    One small brown puppy runs to Jack. It looks at him with big eyes.
    The puppy is happy to see him. It wants to play.
    "I love this one!" says Jack. "Can we take him home, please?"
    Mom and Dad smile. "Yes, you can," they say. Jack is very glad.
    They give the puppy a name. "His name is Rex," says Jack.
    At home, Rex is hungry. He eats his food and drinks some water.
    Then Rex is tired. He sleeps in a soft new bed.
    In the afternoon, Jack plays with Rex in the garden. They run and jump.
    Rex is a good dog. He likes the red ball. He runs after it.
    Every day, Jack feeds Rex in the morning. He walks with him after school.
    Rex is happy, and Jack is happy too.
    Now Jack has a new best friend.
    "I love you, Rex," says Jack. Rex wags his tail.
