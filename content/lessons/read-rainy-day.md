  textEn: |
    It is Saturday morning. It is raining today. The rain is cold and grey. Emma and
    Ben look at the window. They are sad. They can't go outside. They can't play in
    the park today.
    "What can we do?" asks Ben. "I want to play! I don't want to stay inside."
    "Don't be sad," says Mom. "I have a good idea. Let's stay inside and have fun. We
    can do many things at home."
    First, they draw pictures. They use bright pens — red, blue and green. Emma draws
    a black cat. The cat is funny. Ben draws a big blue car. "Look at my car!" says
    Ben. "It is very fast."
    Then they build a house. They use their blocks. They build a tall, tall house.
    "Our house is big!" says Emma. But then the house falls down. Crash! The children
    laugh and laugh.
    Mom makes hot tea. She brings some biscuits too. The biscuits are sweet. The
    children sit on the sofa. They eat and drink. They are happy now.
    After that, Mom reads a funny book. It is a story about a dog and a cat. The
    children laugh a lot. The day is not boring. The day is fun!
    In the evening, the rain stops. The sky is clear and blue. The sun comes out.
    "Look, Mom, no more rain!" says Ben.
    "I like rainy days," says Emma with a big smile. "Rainy days are fun at home."
